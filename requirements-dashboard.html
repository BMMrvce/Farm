FarmOps Requirement Analysis Dashboard

Requirement Modules

Module What is Needed Priority
Voice to Structured Notes Record Kannada/English manager-farmer calls and auto-convert into key points and recommendation report. High
WhatsApp Intelligence Ingest WhatsApp group updates (pest, labor, dispatch, incidents) into searchable, tagged events. High
Ops Dashboard Evening summary dashboard with farm-wise + consolidated view, including pop-up critical alerts. High
Labor and Payment Support Auto-calculate labor counts (ladies/gents), weekly payable summaries, and trace source messages. Medium
Dispatch and Billing Consolidation Track multi-farm flower dispatch and produce farm-level + unified end-of-day billing sheets. Medium

Role Hierarchy Expectations

Farm Supervisor Posts field observations, labor counts, incidents.
Assistant Manager Responds to issues, validates actions, needs quick daily report.
State / HO Leadership Needs consolidated multi-farm visibility.
Accounts / Finance Needs approved records before payment release.

MVP Scope (Zone-Level First)

1. Capture Voice + WhatsApp messages with source metadata (farm, date, person).
2. Extract AI summary: issue, recommendation, due action, owner, severity.
3. Visualize Mobile-first dashboard with unresolved issues and EOD report.
4. Notify Push critical pop-up alerts (water failure, disease, urgent task).

Phase-2 Expansion

A. Finance link Dispatch/billing consolidation and payout-ready summaries.
B. Workflow engine Multi-level approvals and auditability.
C. Agronomy analytics PH/EC trend and feed/consumption quality checks.
D. Integration bridge Sync with existing geo-fenced application where needed.
